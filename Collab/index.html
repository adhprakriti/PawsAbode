Adoption and Fostering

Adoption means providing a shelter dog with a permanent caring home with a family for the rest of its life. We try our best to match dogs with suitable owners and homes, and where possible, undertake follow-up visits to ensure that the dog is well and happy in its environment.

Fostering means providing shelter dogs, particularly puppies, with a temporary but stable and caring home with a family for a few months. This provides the dog with individual care until such time as it is fully vaccinated, adopted, or the foster carer must move on
